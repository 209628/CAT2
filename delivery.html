Delivery & Transportation

Reliable delivery of fencing materials across Eldoret and beyond

Our Delivery Service

Harmony Homewise offers reliable transportation and delivery services to ensure fencing materials reach customers safely and on time. We understand that timely delivery is critical to keeping your project on schedule, which is why we have invested in dependable transport solutions.

We serve clients within Eldoret and surrounding regions, delivering chain link fencing, barbed wire, fence posts, and accessories directly to your site.

How Our Delivery Works

  • Step 1 — Place Your Order: Contact us with your fencing material requirements.
  • Step 2 — Site Measurement: Our team can visit your site to confirm exact quantities needed.
  • Step 3 — Material Preparation: Your order is prepared and loaded for transport.
  • Step 4 — Delivery: Materials are delivered safely to your site within the agreed timeframe.
  • Step 5 — Offloading Support: Our team assists with offloading materials at your site.

Areas We Cover

We currently provide delivery services within Eldoret town and the wider Uasin Gishu County, including surrounding agricultural and residential areas. For larger orders, we also arrange transportation to other regions in Kenya upon request.
