Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our products and services

General Questions

1. What does Harmony Homewise do?

Harmony Homewise is a fencing and construction solutions company based in Eldoret, Kenya. We supply and install high-quality chain link fencing materials for residential, commercial, agricultural, and industrial projects.

2. Where are you located?

We are located in Eldoret, Uasin Gishu County, Kenya. We serve clients within Eldoret and surrounding regions.

3. What products do you sell?

We sell chain link fencing in both large and small gauge, barbed wire, fence posts, and other fencing accessories such as nails.

Pricing & Payment

4. How much does chain link fencing cost?

Our chain link fencing prices depend on the height and gauge selected. Light gauge starts from KES 2,200 for 4 feet, while heavy gauge starts from KES 2,600 for 4 feet. Visit our Price List page for full details.

5. Do prices include delivery?

Delivery within Eldoret town is free. For areas outside Eldoret, a delivery fee is charged based on distance. Visit our Delivery page for more information.

6. Can I get a custom quote?

Yes. Contact us directly with your project details and our team will provide a tailored quote based on your specific requirements.

Installation & Services

7. Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we have a professional installation team that handles chain link installation, site measurements, and custom fencing solutions.

8. How long does installation take?

Installation time depends on the size and complexity of the project. Our team will provide an estimated timeline after a site visit and consultation.

9. Do you offer maintenance and repair services?

Yes, we provide after-sale support including maintenance and repair services to keep your fence in excellent condition.

Delivery & Coverage

10. What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver within Eldoret town and the wider Uasin Gishu County. For larger orders, we can arrange transportation to other regions in Kenya upon request.

11. How can I contact you?

You can reach us by phone at +254 705 118 064, by email at harmonyhomewise@gmail.com, or by visiting our Contact Us page.
