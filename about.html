About Us

Learn more about Harmony Homewise and our mission

Who We Are

Harmony Homewise Link Investment is a Kenya-based manufacturing company specializing in chain link fencing and related security products. We manufacture and supply durable chain links, barbed wires, nails and fencing materials for residential, commercial and industrual applications.

Strong durable, and trusted chain links for every fencing project across the country.

Our Mission

To provide durable, affordable, and reliable fencing solutions while maintaining excellent customer service and professionalism.

Our Vision

To become the leading fencing and chain link supplier in Kenya by offering quality products and trusted services.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity — We are honest and transparent in all our dealings.
  • Quality Assurance — We only supply and install materials that meet high standards.
  • Customer Satisfaction — Our clients' needs always come first.
  • Reliability — We deliver on our promises, every time.
  • Professionalism — We conduct ourselves with the highest level of professionalism.
  • Innovation — We continuously look for better ways to serve our clients.
