Welcome to Harmony Homewise

Harmony Homewise is a trusted fencing and construction solutions company based in Eldoret, Kenya. We specialize in the supply and installation of high-quality chain link fencing materials for residential, commercial, agricultural, and industrial projects.

Strong, durable and trusted for every fencing project across the country. Your security starts with quality.

Our high-quality chain link fencing - durable, secure and affordable.

Why Customers Trust Us?

  • High-quality and durable fencing materials — Built to last in all weather conditions.
  • Affordable and competitive prices — Quality fencing that fits your budget.
  • Professional installation team — Experienced and skilled workers on every project.
  • Timely delivery and transportation — We serve Eldoret and surrounding regions.
  • Honest customer service — Transparent pricing and reliable communication.
  • Reliable after-sale support — We are here even after the job is done.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of fencing services including chain link installation, fencing project consultation, transportation and delivery, site measurements, custom fencing solutions, and maintenance and repairs.
